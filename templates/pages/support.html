Privacy policy

Decide what type of activity you want to store in your account. For example, if you turn on the "PandoraBox History" setting, your video viewing history and search history will be stored in your account to provide you with more relevant recommendations and remember your video playback progress. If you turn on the "Web & App Activity" setting, your searches and activity on other services will be saved to your account to provide services that are more tailored to your needs, such as improving search efficiency and recommending more useful apps and content.
